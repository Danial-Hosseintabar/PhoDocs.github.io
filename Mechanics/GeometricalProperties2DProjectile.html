Geometrical Properties of 2D projectile movement


Assume a moving projectile in the 2D plane of x-y, thown with an initial velocity \(v_0\) which makes an angle \(theta\) with the x-axis. Differential equations describing the motions are:
\[y=\frac{-gt^2}{2}+v_0sin(\theta)t+y_0\] \[x = v_0cos(\theta)t + x_0\] and the trajectory is a parabola with equation: \[y=\frac{-gx^2}{2v_0^2cos^2(\theta)}+xtan(\theta)+y_0\]
